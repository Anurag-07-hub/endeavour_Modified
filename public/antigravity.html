Gesture 1: Scroll
Scroll Down: Point your index finger straight up (other fingers closed).
Scroll Up: Point index & middle fingers straight up (peace sign).

Linear Interpolation

The scrolling utilizes a LERP function on the Y-axis. The raw hand coordinates are smoothed out heavily to prevent jitter and emulate a frictionless vacuum environment.

Gesture 2: Tab Switching
Forward: Right hand starts half-closed, then rapidly flicks outward to a flat straight hand. Do this twice quickly.
Backward: Right hand starts flat/straight, then curls inward twice quickly.

Cooldown Protocol

A strict 1500ms cooldown is applied after a successful tab switch to prevent accidental multi-page skips.

State Machine
